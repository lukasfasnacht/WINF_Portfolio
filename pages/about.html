Who I am

whatever man

I grew up in a small town called Bonstetten, just outside of Zurich. Which I still call my home till now. All my family and friends still live there as well. I spent most of my childhood outdoors with my brother. Whether it was football, MTB or snowboarding, as long as it was fun. Until now, sports and just being outside is what fulfills me the most.

studies

After successfully completing the vocational baccalaureate, I was still very unsure of the direction I would like to take in the future. It was clear to me that I did not want to stay in the pure computer science, my goal was to live out my creativity in the profession. Things like UX and UI design already interested me during my apprenticeship. After an intensive analysis of the different study programs I stumbled upon the study program DBM at the FHGR. It was quickly clear to me that my search had come to an end. Topics like digital innovation, information technology and user experience will become more and more important and complex in the future. My goal after graduation is to actively participate in the innovation of the Internet.
