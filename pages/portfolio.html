Some of my previous projects

Lernende-Page

I designed a landing page for the apprentice team of Swisscom's Service Obligation. The landing page creates an overview of the current apprentices, as well as former apprentices. Since the apprentices are daily on the road with new technicians, this page ensures that the technicians have a picture in advance of the apprentices. In addition, their commitment to the Service Obligation shall be recognized. Every apprentice gets the opportunity to design their own portfolio.

RTT-Demo

In the context of a presentation about future satellite technologies for the board of directors of Swisscom, I created a website that shows the loading time of a complex website with different elements. This was to show in a simplified way what effect the round trip time has on the loading time of web pages, independent of the internet speed.

Unfortunately the website is currently offline due to maintenance. You can still download the source code on GitHub though.

Connect to WLAN

Together with the RTT-Demo website, I have created a website that allows you to quickly and easily connect to an appropriate WLAN via QR codes. As part of the presentation there was a demo part where you could connect to different satellite technologies, with different round trip times.
